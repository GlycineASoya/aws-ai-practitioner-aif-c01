# Gen AI Business Value

## Business Metrics

1. Performance
   1. Cross-domain performance (chat-bot)
   2. The higher the better
2. Efficiency
   1. Speed and Resource consumption
   2. The faster speed and the lower resource consumption the better
3. Conversion rate
   1. The conversion rate of % of users who took a certain action after had a talk with AI
   2. The higher the conversion rate the better
4. Average Revenue per User (ARPU)
   1. Financial metric, represents revenue per user over a period
   2. The more the better
5. Accuracy
   1. Correct and desired output
   2. THe higher the better
6. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
   1. Total business revenue per user over an entire relationship
   2. The more the better
