# Exam guide

1. Machine Learning (ML)
2. Artificial Intellegence (AI)
3. Generative AI

## Exam Content

1. 65 questions
2. 90 mins to answer the questions
3. 4-6 Mulptiple choice
4. NO Partial Credit (correct answer is counted if all the correct answer for multiple choice questions are marked)
5. Score required 700/1000
6. 15 Unscored questions (variable scores based on the question complexity)
7. NO penalty for guessing (good luck!)

## Exam Topics

1. 20% out of 65 questions - Fundamentals of AI and ML
   1. explain basic AI concepts and terminology
   2. identify practical use cases for AI
   3. describe the ML SDLC
2. 24% out of 65 questions - Fundamentals of GenAI
   1. explain the basic concepts of GenAI
   2. understand the capabilities and limitations of GenAI for solving business challenges
   3. describe AWS infrastructure and technologies for building GenAI applications
3. 28% out of 65 questions - Applications of Foundation Models
   1. describe design considerations for applications that use foundation models
   2. choose effective prompt engineering techniques
   3. desribe the training and fine-tuning process for the models
   4. describe methods to evaluate model performance
4. 14% out of 65 questions - Guidelines for Responsible AI
   1. explain the development of AI systems that are responsible
   2. recognize the importance of transparent and explainable models
5. 14% out of 65 questions - Security, Complience, and Governance for AI solutions
   1. explain methods to secure AI systems
   2. recognize governance and compliance regualtion for AI systems
